Top Nutrition Tips

1. Eat More Whole Foods

Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid highly processed foods when possible.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is crucial for digestion, energy, and overall health. Aim for at least 8 glasses a day.

Quick Healthy Recipes

Avocado Toast

Toast whole-grain bread, smash an avocado, sprinkle salt, pepper, and top with a fried egg.

Fruit Smoothie

Blend banana, spinach, berries, and almond milk for a refreshing, nutrient-rich drink.
